About Us Web-450 NodeBucket is a task manager application that was developed for the Web-450 course at Bellevue University. The purpose of the application is to demonstrate our knowledge of the MEAN stack. This application utilizes all components of the MEAN stack, MongoDB, Express, Angular, and Node.js. The application was developed using agile development practices. We developed this project over the course of four weeks. One week was designated for design and was followed by three one week sprints.
